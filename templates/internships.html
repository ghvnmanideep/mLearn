Internships at mLearn
Unlock Your Potential with mLearn Internships!

At mLearn, we believe in the power of education and the importance of hands-on experience. Our internship program is designed to provide students and recent graduates with the opportunity to gain valuable skills, contribute to meaningful projects, and be part of a dynamic team dedicated to transforming education.

Why Intern with Us?

  • Real-World Experience: Work on projects that make a difference in the lives of learners. Gain practical experience in the education sector and enhance your resume.
  • Mentorship: Collaborate with experienced professionals who are passionate about education. Receive guidance and feedback to help you grow in your career.
  • Flexible Learning Environment: Enjoy a supportive and flexible work environment that encourages creativity and innovation.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals, fellow interns, and educators who share your passion for learning.

Internship Opportunities
We offer a variety of internship positions across different departments. Here are some of the areas where you can contribute:

Content Development Intern
Assist in creating engaging educational materials, including articles, videos, and interactive content.
Collaborate with educators to ensure content aligns with learning objectives.

Marketing Intern
Help promote mLearn through social media, email campaigns, and other marketing strategies.
Conduct market research to identify trends and opportunities for growth.

Technical Support Intern
Provide assistance to users experiencing technical issues with the mLearn platform.
Work with the development team to improve user experience and troubleshoot problems.

Research Intern
Conduct research on educational trends, technologies, and best practices.
Analyze data to support program development and improvement initiatives.

Internship Details
  • Duration: Internships typically last 3-6 months, with flexible start dates.
  • Location: Remote and in-person opportunities available, depending on the position.
  • Compensation: Interns may receive a stipend or academic credit, depending on the role and institution.

How to Apply
Ready to take the next step in your career? Here’s how to apply for an internship at mLearn:

  • Prepare Your Application: Include your resume, a cover letter detailing your interest in the position, and any relevant work samples or portfolios.
  • Submit Your Application: Fill out the application form here with the subject line “Internship Application – [Position Name].”
  • Interview Process: Selected candidates will be contacted for an interview. We look forward to learning more about you!
